Prepare the liquid medium. Dispense the medium into culture vessels. Sterilize by
autoclaving. Before inoculation add from sterile stock solutions:

KNO3                                                               1.0       g
Glucose                                                            2.0       g
Vitamin Solution (see medium141)                                   1.0      ml
NaHCO3 (8% solution)                                              12.5      ml

The 8% NaHCO3 solution may be either filter sterilized or autoclaved. When the solution
is autoclaved use tightly closed vessels that are filled to a maximum of 50% of the total
volume.



The final pH of the medium should be adjusted to pH 7.6-8.0.
