1475. METALLIBACTERIUM MEDIUM


 (NH4)2SO4                                                        132.0    mg
 MgCl2 x 6 H2O                                                     53.0    mg
 KH2PO4                                                            27.0    mg
 CaCl2 x 2 H2O                                                    147.0    mg
 VOSO4 x 5 H2O                                                      0.5    mg
 Wolfe’s mineral elixier (see medium 792)                           1.0    ml
 FeSO4 x 7 H2O                                                     10.0    mg
 Trypticase peptone (BD BBL)                                        1.4     g
 Vitamins solution (see medium 141)                                10.0    ml
 Distilled water                                                 1000.0    ml

Dissolve ingredients except vitamins and ferrous sulfate and adjust pH of solution to 5.0.
Dissolve ferrous sulfate (1% w/v) in 0.1 N H2SO4 and add to the medium prior to
autoclaving. After autoclaving add vitamins from a stock solution that has been sterilized
by filtration.
