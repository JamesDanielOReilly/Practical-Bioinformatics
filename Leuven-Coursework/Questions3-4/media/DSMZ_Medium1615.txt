1615. Basic mineral medium


20 mM K-P-Buffer:
0.1 M K2HPO4 130 ml
0.1 M KH2PO4 70 ml
a. dest      800 ml
pH 7.0-7.2

Sterilize at 121C for 20 minutes in 1L Schott bottle

After sterilization add from sterile stock solutions:
 1.0 ml/l 1 M Mg SO4x7H2O (1 mM),
10.0ml/l 1 M sodium acetate (10 mM),
 1.0 ml/l 5% CaCl2x2H2O (might result in colloid formation, especially when added into
hot medium, but dissolves after vigorous shaking),
 1.0 ml/l 2 M sodium aspartate (2 mM),
 1.0 ml/l Trace elements (see below),
 0.5 ml/l Vitamins ( see below) and
 1.0 ml/l 50 mM L-methionine, filter sterilized, neutralised (50 µM).
