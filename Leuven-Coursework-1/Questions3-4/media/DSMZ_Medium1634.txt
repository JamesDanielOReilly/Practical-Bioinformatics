Adjust the pH to 11 with NaOH. Fill into to screw-cap tubes, leaving 60% headspace and
flush headspace with N2. Add 30% air and for DSM 103920 additionally add 50% H2.

Autoclave medium for 20 min at 121°C, cool and add from sterile stock solutions while
stirring:
0.3 mM K2HPO4 (1ml of 300 mM stock solution per liter)
5 mM CaCl2x2 H2O (5ml of 1 M stock solution per liter)
4 mM Na-acetate (2ml of 2 M stock solution per liter)
Vitamin solution 10 ml per liter
Trace element solution 10 ml per liter
Some precipitate will form.
