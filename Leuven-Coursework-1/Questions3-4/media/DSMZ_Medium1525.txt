1525. KALLOTENUE MEDIUM


 HEPES buffer (SIGMA)                                               3.57  g
 NH4Cl                                                             27.00 mg
 CaSO4 x 2 H2O                                                     60.00 mg
 MgSO4 x 7 H2O                                                      0.10  g
 NaCl                                                               8.00 mg
 KNO3                                                               0.10  g
 NaNO3                                                              0.69  g
 Na2HPO4                                                            0.11  g
 Trace elements solution (see medium 141)                          10.00 ml
 D-Glucose                                                          2.00  g
 Trypticase peptone (BD BBL)                                        1.00  g
 Vitamin solution (see medium 141)                                 10.00 ml
 Distilled water                                                 1000.00 ml

Dissolve ingredients (except glucose, peptone and vitamins) and adjust pH to 8.1.
Dispense medium under air atmosphere into culture vessels suitable for anaerobic
cultivation (e.g., 10 ml medium per Balch-type tube), seal and autoclave. Add glucose,
peptone and vitamins from sterile stock solutions sterilized by filtration. Adjust pH of
complete medium to 7.5, if necessary.
